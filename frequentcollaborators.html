Johnny Depp

Johnny Depp has been in the following Tim Burton films:
Edward Scissorhands (1990)
Ed Wood (1994)
Sleepy Hollow (1999)
Charlie and the Chocolate Factory (2005)
Corpse Bride (2005) (voice)
Sweeney Todd: The Demon Barber of Fleet Street (2007)
Alice in Wonderland (2010)
Dark Shadows (2012)
Johnny Depp was also in Alice Through the Looking Glass (2016), which Tim Burton produced but did not direct.

Danny Elfman

Working on Pee-Wee's Big Adventure, Burton and Elfman developed an immediate rapport. Since then, they have worked their magic on Beetlejuice, Batman and Batman Returns, Edward Scissorhands , The Nightmare Before Christmas, Mars Attacks!, Planet of the Apes , Corpse Bride, Charlie and the Chocolate Factory, Dark Shadows, Sleepy Hallow, 9, and Big Fish.
